Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026‑05‑05

NightSky Snaps is a static website that publishes information from NASA’s Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD). We don’t run user accounts or comments.

What we collect

  • Server logs (via GitHub Pages/CDN): standard request data and IPs for security/operations.
  • Advertising (Google AdSense, if enabled): may use cookies or similar tech to serve and measure ads. See Google’s policies.
  • Donations (Ko‑fi / Buy Me a Coffee): if you tip, those platforms process your payment and may collect information.
  • Store (Gumroad/Payhip/Redbubble): if you purchase, those platforms process your order and may collect information.
  • Links: outbound links to merchants may be affiliate links.

Cookies

We don’t set our own cookies. Third‑party services (e.g., AdSense) may set cookies for ad delivery/measurement. You can control cookies in your browser.

Third‑party services

  • Hosting: GitHub Pages (Microsoft/GitHub)
  • Ads: Google AdSense (Google)
  • Donations: Ko‑fi and/or Buy Me a Coffee
  • Store: Gumroad/Payhip/Redbubble (as linked on our Store page)

Children’s privacy

This site is general‑audience content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the site owner via their GitHub profile.
