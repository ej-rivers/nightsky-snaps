About

NightSky Snaps automatically publishes NASA’s Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) with quick context, an archive, and helpful links for stargazers.

APOD content and copyrights vary by day. NASA/ESA imagery is often public domain; some third‑party images are not. Always check the source link on each page.
